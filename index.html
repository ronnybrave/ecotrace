AI-Powered Analysis

Satellite Greenery Intelligence

Our AI compares satellite imagery over time, detecting vegetation growth with precision unavailable to ground teams.

01
Project Registration
NGOs register plantation sites with GPS coordinates, planting dates, and species information.
02
Baseline Satellite Capture
Pre-plantation imagery is captured and stored as the baseline for future comparisons.
03
AI Vegetation Analysis
NDVI and machine learning models analyze spectral data to detect live vegetation and canopy growth.
04
Periodic Monitoring
Automated scans every 3 months track survival rates, growth velocity, and seasonal health.
05
Impact Report Generation
Comprehensive PDF reports with carbon absorption, survival rate, and area coverage metrics.
